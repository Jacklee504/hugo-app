The best categories for a small deals site to own

March 26, 2026

A small deals site does not need to cover the whole internet to feel useful. In most cases, breadth is what makes these projects look thin.

The better approach is to choose categories that reward repeat browsing and straightforward comparisons. Audio, home office gear, running tech, and compact fitness equipment all work well because shoppers usually have clear intent and price matters.

That creates a stronger editorial loop:

  • readers understand what the site is good at
  • category pages feel deeper faster
  • email alerts become more relevant because they map to real interests

Over time, that focus is what helps a project feel like a trusted niche brand instead of a generic affiliate feed.
