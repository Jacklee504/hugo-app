How we decide a discount is worth featuring

March 30, 2026

Not every percentage-off badge deserves attention. A useful deals site needs a filter, otherwise the homepage turns into noise.

For this project, the baseline is:

  • a product has to belong to a category the site actually covers
  • the discount has to be understandable in context, not just eye-catching
  • the product should have a clear use case for the person browsing that category
  • the page needs a short explanation of why the item matters

That approach makes the site feel more like a recommendation brand than a feed of random links. Even before live retailer integrations are connected, this editorial layer is what gives the project credibility.
