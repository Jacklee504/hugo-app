Why category alerts matter more than a generic newsletter

March 28, 2026

Most deal newsletters try to cover everything at once. That is convenient for publishers, but it is usually bad for readers.

Someone shopping for headphones does not want kitchen gadgets mixed into the same email. Someone building a home gym probably does not care about portable speakers. Category alerts solve that problem by letting readers opt into exactly the topics they care about.

For a site like Deal Ledger, that creates a better experience in two ways:

  • readers get fewer, more relevant emails
  • the site can build stronger topic authority over time because each category has its own audience and content rhythm

Even in this pre-launch sample, the alerts page exists to demonstrate that future direction.
