About

March 31, 2026

Deal Ledger is a pre-launch sample site built around a simple idea: a deals website should be useful even before a reader clicks out to a retailer.

The project is being designed to focus on:

  • curated product picks instead of giant dumps of coupons
  • clear price context, including reference pricing and discount depth
  • category-specific email alerts so readers can follow the topics they actually care about
  • lightweight editorial guides that explain why a product or category is worth watching

This version of the site exists to demonstrate structure, tone, and user experience before live retailer integrations are connected. The core goal is to launch with enough depth and clarity to be a trustworthy recommendation property, not just an empty affiliate shell.
